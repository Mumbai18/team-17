Patient Housing

Staying is a challenge in a big city like Mumbai and expensive too. V Care Foundation supports the patients stay. Rs.22,000/- is paid towards 3 patients stay and gas usage at ACTREC.

Wigs

Available for a subsidized donation of Rs. 3000/- for patients and some are given free depending on the category of the file. Confidence Bags (patients are given bags made by V Care to keep their drainage bags): Given to all patients who have had surgery to keep their drainage tubes and suction containers. Around 50 bags are given in a month, each bag cost Rs. 25/-. Patient Transport: Travelling is a challenge in a city like Mumbai and if one is a patient and is new and has to do the trip to hospital it becomes even more challenging. To ease this difficulty and also to save on some expense on behalf of the patients. V Care has its vans who ferry Patients and caregivers who stay at Dharamshala at Dadar every day to hospital for their treatment.

Prosthesis for Breast Cancer Patients

Breast prosthesis for patient who have undergone mastectomy. These are cotton prosthesis which are washable and suitable to the Indian climate.
